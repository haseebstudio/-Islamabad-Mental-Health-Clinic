What We Offer

Our Clinical Services

We offer a full range of mental health services — from initial assessment to long-term support — tailored to each patient's unique journey.

Psychiatric Evaluation

Comprehensive assessments to understand your mental health condition, followed by a personalised treatment plan developed with care and precision.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions with our licensed therapists using evidence-based approaches including CBT, DBT, and mindfulness-based therapy tailored to your needs.

Family Counselling

Supporting families through difficult times — helping improve communication, resolve conflict, and build stronger, healthier relationships at home.

Child & Adolescent Care

Specialised support for children and teenagers dealing with behavioural challenges, academic stress, anxiety, or emotional difficulties.

Medication Management

Safe, monitored psychiatric medication prescribed and regularly reviewed by our qualified psychiatrists to ensure effectiveness and your wellbeing.

Online Consultations

Can't come in person? We offer secure, private video sessions so you can access the same quality care from the comfort of your own space.

About IMHC

A Clinic That Truly Cares

IMHC — Islamabad Mental Health Clinic — was founded with a simple but powerful belief: that mental health care should be accessible, dignified, and genuinely effective for everyone. Located conveniently on the Main Bahria Expressway near Globe Roundabout, Bahria Town, we serve patients from across Islamabad and Rawalpindi.

Our team of qualified psychiatrists, psychologists, and counsellors work together to offer holistic, person-centred care. We understand that reaching out for help takes courage, and we're committed to making every visit feel safe, supportive, and completely confidential.

Compassionate, Judgment-Free Care Every patient is treated with empathy, respect, and dignity — no matter what brings them to us.
Evidence-Based Treatment Our practitioners follow internationally recognised clinical standards and the latest research in mental health.
Complete Confidentiality Your privacy is our priority. Everything shared within our clinic stays within our clinic.
